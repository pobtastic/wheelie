Infinite Lives
To gain infinite lives:
POKE InitialiseGame_0,C9
Just return, instead of decreasing Lives by one:
InitialiseGame_0 LD HL,7839
7555 LD (HL)
Infinite Fuel
For infinite fuel:
POKE 76AC,2A
POKE E86C,3A
Fuel is held by 783C and 783D. This changes the following two instances where the fuel is decreased.
Altering where the decreased fuel is written back to Fuel:
76AA SBC HL,DE
76AC LD (783C),HL
To LoaD the value into HL instead of writing it to Fuel:
76AC LD HL,(783C)
And similarly, altering:
E867 LD A,783D
E86A SUB 08
E86C LD (783D),A
For a similar outcome (LoaDing into A instead of writing to 783D:
E86C LD A,(783D)
