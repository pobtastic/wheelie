E891: Displays the Start Screen
Sets the top two rows of the attribute buffer to magenta paper with white ink.
PINK_HEADER E891 LD HL,$5800 Writes $1F (Magenta/ White) to the top two rows of the attribute buffer.
E894 LD B,$40
PINK_HEADER_LOOP E896 LD (HL),$1F
E898 INC L
E899 DJNZ PINK_HEADER_LOOP
Source is currently $5840, we write $06 (Black/Yellow) to the memory location held in the source register. Target is then just "source+1", so the LDIR just fast copies into the following $01BF memory locations (up to $59FF accounting for the top two-thirds of the screen).
CLEAR_START_SCREEN E89B LD D,H Write $06 (Black/ Yellow) to the next $01BF memory locations of the attribute buffer
E89C LD E,L
E89D INC E
E89E LD BC,$01BF
E8A1 LD (HL),$06
E8A3 LDIR
E8A5 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
From here onward fills the screen buffer with text/ images. Starting with the "welcome" text.
E8A6 LD DE,$4000 DE=$4000 (top of the screen buffer)
E8A9 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8AA LD HL,$E871 Point HL to WELCOME_TEXT ($20 characters in length) and call PRINT_STRING
E8AD LD B,$20
E8AF CALL PRINT_STRING
This section handles the cute row of bike graphics in the top of the display.
E8B2 LD B,$10 B=$10 (number of bike UDGs to display)
BANNER_BIKE_GRAPHIC_LOOP E8B4 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8B5 LD L,$9C Point HL to BIKE_GRAPHIC and call PRINT_BIKE_GRAPHIC this prints the left half of the bike graphic
E8B7 CALL PRINT_BIKE_GRAPHIC
E8BA EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8BB CALL PRINT_BIKE_GRAPHIC Calling the subroutine PRINT_BIKE_GRAPHIC again prints the right half of the bike graphic
E8BE DJNZ BANNER_BIKE_GRAPHIC_LOOP Decrease bike counter by one, jump back to BANNER_BIKE_GRAPHIC_LOOP if not zero
This loop prints the "start screen text" in blocks of $40 characters (two rows at a time).
E8C0 LD C,$04 C=$04 (there are 4 blocks of text to display)
E8C2 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8C3 LD DE,$4060 DE=$4060 (position in screen buffer we're writing to)
E8C6 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL
E8C7 LD HL,$A800 Point HL to START_SCREEN_TEXT ($40 characters in length) and call PRINT_STRING
START_SCREEN_TEXT_LOOP E8CA LD B,$40
E8CC CALL PRINT_STRING
E8CF EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8D0 LD A,E Meh
E8D1 AND A
E8D2 JR NZ,START_SCREEN_TEXT_SKIP
E8D4 LD D,$48
START_SCREEN_TEXT_SKIP E8D6 ADD A,$20
E8D8 LD E,A
E8D9 EXX Exchange BC, DE, and HL with shadow registers with BC', DE', and HL'
E8DA DEC C
E8DB JR NZ,START_SCREEN_TEXT_LOOP
E8DD JP PAUSE Jump to PAUSE
